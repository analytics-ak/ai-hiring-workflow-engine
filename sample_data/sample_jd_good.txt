## Senior Data Analyst | Full-Time | Bangalore

### About the Role
You will own the analytics infrastructure that powers decisions across product, 
marketing, and finance teams. This role exists because we are scaling from 
50,000 to 500,000 users and need someone to turn growing data into clear insights.

### What You Will Do
- Build and maintain Power BI dashboards tracking user behavior and revenue metrics 
  for C-suite and department heads
- Write complex SQL queries to extract and transform data from PostgreSQL databases 
  for weekly business reviews
- Develop Python scripts to automate reporting tasks, reducing manual work by 15+ 
  hours per week
- Analyze A/B test results for product features and present findings to product managers
- Identify trends in customer churn and recommend data-backed interventions
- Collaborate with engineering to define tracking requirements for new features

### What You Bring
REQUIRED:
- 3-5 years experience as a data analyst or business analyst
- Strong SQL including joins, subqueries, and window functions
- Hands-on experience building dashboards in Power BI or similar BI tools
- Working knowledge of Python for data analysis (pandas, numpy)
- Ability to translate technical findings for non-technical stakeholders

PREFERRED:
- Prior experience in fintech or financial services
- Familiarity with A/B testing methodologies
- Experience with Git for version control
- Advanced Excel including pivot tables and VLOOKUP

### What We Offer
Salary range of Rs 12-18 LPA based on experience plus ESOP grant vesting over 
4 years. Health insurance covering you, spouse, and children. Flexible hours 
with 3 days in-office and 2 days remote. Annual learning budget of Rs 50,000.

### How to Apply
Send your resume and a brief note about your most impactful data project to 
careers@company.com with Data Analyst Bangalore in the subject line.
